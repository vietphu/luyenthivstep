(B) The AEC will be equivalent to the world's seventh-largest economy. It was set up to create a highly competitive single market and production area. Organizers hope it will ease the movement of capital, goods, investment, services, and skilled labor across ASEAN countries. (C) But some business leaders have low expectations for the ASEAN summit. Anthony Nelson is a director at the US-ASEAN Business Council in Washington.
